# New Hire Onboarding Guide

## Setting your password
Set your account password once during onboarding. Your password never expires, so you won't need to change it again.

## Multi-factor authentication
MFA is optional during your first week while you get set up. You can enable it later from your account settings whenever you are ready.

## Working remotely
When working from home, connect through the company VPN before accessing internal tools.
