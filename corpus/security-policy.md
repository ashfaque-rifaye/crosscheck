# Information Security Policy

## Password rotation
All user account passwords must be rotated at least once every 90 days. Accounts whose passwords exceed this age are automatically locked until the password is changed.

## Multi-factor authentication
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for every employee accessing internal systems, without exception.

## Audit log retention
Security audit logs are retained for 7 years to satisfy compliance obligations.

## Remote access
Employees connecting from outside the corporate network must use the company VPN.
