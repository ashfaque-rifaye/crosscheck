# Master Services Agreement

## Refund window
The Customer may request a refund within 14 days of the invoice date. No refunds are issued after this period.

## Service level commitment
The Provider commits to 99.5% monthly uptime. Service credits apply if uptime falls below this threshold.

## Billing terms
Fees are invoiced annually in advance.
